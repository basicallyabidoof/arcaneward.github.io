About Us

Sean Juroviesky is a leading cybersecurity strategist who brings a decade of front-line experience from the tech, medical, and non-profit sectors to the main stage. As a senior cybersecurity architect for the global music platform SoundCloud, they don't just talk about theory they live on the front lines of cyber defense every day.

Sean is a rare "translator" who can bridge the gap between the highly technical world (as a respected speaker at elite cons like DEF CON and Blue Team Con) and the executive boardroom. Sean excels at demystifying complex threats and transforming security from a technical cost center into a core business enabler. Their keynotes focus on practical strategy, cultural change, and aligning security with business outcomes.

Conferences

An incomplete list of conferences I attended and can strongly recommend.

Cyphercon

A hacker conference in Wisconsin, focused on a wide range of cybersecurity topics, with a friendly and welcoming atmosphere.

Def Con

One of the world's largest and most famous hacker conventions, held annually in Las Vegas. It's known for its cutting-edge research and Capture the Flag competition.

Sec-T

A technical security conference in Stockholm, Sweden, that focuses on providing a friendly and open environment for knowledge sharing.

DrivingIT

A conference by the Danish IT Society, focusing on the latest trends and challenges in IT.

FWD:CloudSec Europe

A non-profit conference in Europe focused on cloud security, with a mission to share knowledge and experience among professionals.

ChiBrrCon

A Chicago-based cybersecurity conference that takes place in the winter, offering a smaller, more intimate setting for learning and networking.

CornCon

A cybersecurity conference in the Quad Cities (Iowa/Illinois) with a fun, corn-themed atmosphere, aimed at educating and bringing together the local security community.

SecretCon

An intimate cybersecurity conference focusing on practical, hands-on knowledge sharing.

BSides312

A community-driven, low-cost cybersecurity conference held in Chicago, part of the global Security BSides movement.

ThotCon

A hacking conference in Chicago that provides a platform for security professionals and enthusiasts to share knowledge and research.

BSides Rochester

A BSides event in Rochester, New York, offering a day of security talks and networking for the local community.

BSides Las Vegas

One of the largest BSides events, held during "Hacker Summer Camp" in Las Vegas, offering a wide range of talks and workshops.
