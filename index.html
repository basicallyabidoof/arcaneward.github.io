About Us

Sean Juroviesky is a leading cybersecurity strategist who brings a decade of front-line experience from the tech, medical, and non-profit sectors to the main stage. Sean is a rare "translator" who can bridge the gap between the highly technical world (as a respected speaker at elite cons like DEF CON and Blue Team Con) and the executive boardroom. Sean excels at demystifying complex threats and transforming security from a technical cost center into a core business enabler. Their keynotes focus on practical strategy, cultural change, and aligning security with business outcomes.
